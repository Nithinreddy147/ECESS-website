ABOUT US

The Electronics & Communication Engineering Students’ Society (ECESS) is a non - profit society led by the students of the Department of Electronics & Communication Engineering, NIT Durgapur. Society is formed to make the students understand and develop professional and technical skills to meet the growing industrial challenges.

OBJECTIVE of ECESS

To inculcate technical skills in students and make them industry-ready.
To form a strong alumni network.
To host technical events to nurture interest in students.
